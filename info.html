It's important to follow the recycling guidelines of the area that you live in. To view Ann Arbor's recycling guidelines, check them out here: https://www.recycleannarbor.org/divisions/curbside-recycling. It's important to check the recycling symbols on what you are throwing out before you assume it is safe to recycle.

If you are curious about what can be composted in the on-campus bins or your own personal one, check out Ann Arbor's composting guidelines here: https://www.a2gov.org/departments/trash-recycling/Pages/Compost.aspx. Composting doesn't have to happen in a backyard, it can look just like a trash can or can even be placed in your freezer to prevent any smells.
